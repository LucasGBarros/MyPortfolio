About me

My name is Lucas Gomes de Barros, and I'm 24 years old. My journey with technology began when I first got my hands on a PlayStation 1 console, which sparked my passion for all things tech. Over the years, as technology evolved, I delved deeper into its intricacies, particularly in the realm of bug discovery and troubleshooting.

My first encounter with bugs came while playing the renowned game "GRAND THEFT AUTO: San Andreas". Through navigating the game's storyline, I began noticing glitches that could either enhance or hinder gameplay. This discovery fueled my fascination with bug finding, eventually leading me to realize the existence of a specialized field for bug hunting and correction during my college years.

Through diligent study and dedication, I secured a position in the QA field, where I applied my skills to identify bugs in banking applications and resolve issues within the financial market. I spent 1 year and 8 months working in this capacity before transitioning to a new challenge a 3-month stint in an artificial intelligence project focused on industrial monitoring.

Currently, I reside in Ireland as part of an exchange program aimed at refining my English language and speaking skills. Additionally, I'm studying Spanish to broaden my linguistic abilities.

I'm deeply passionate about exploring diverse cultures, meeting new people, and maintaining a curious mindset. This drive keeps me constantly evolving and focused on pursuing my goals and beliefs.
